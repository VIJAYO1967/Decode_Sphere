About

Decode Sphere is a signal processing technique used in multiple-input multiple-output (MIMO) communication systems. It combines sphere decoding and lattice reduction techniques to efficiently detect transmitted signals in a high-dimensional space, minimizing errors and improving communication reliability.

Sphere Decoding

A method to search for the most probable transmitted signal within hypersphere around the signal, based on the desired error probability.

Lattice Reduction:

A technique to transform the original complex lattice a reduced lattice with smaller volume, reducing the computational complexity of sphere decoding.

Symbol Estimation

The process of finding the closest point in the reduced lattice to the received signal, representing the most likely transmitted signal within the sphere.
